    Pathway Candidacy

    Weekend: Can Patient Wait?

    Can the patient tolerate a 24-48 hour wait until the LVP (e.g., until Monday morning)?

    ED Action Complete: Schedule Return & Discharge

    Place an order for "US Paracentesis". Call the Sonographer at 4-1383 to notify.

    Provide the patient with discharge instructions, including the arrival time for their 7am Monday LVP. The ED portion of the pathway is complete.

    Action: Symptomatic Relief

    The ED provider should perform a short LVP of approximately 2-3L for symptomatic relief.

    Symptom Check

    After the short LVP, have the patient's symptoms improved enough for discharge and follow-up?

    ED Action Complete: Hold for Morning Procedure

    The patient's symptoms have not sufficiently improved for discharge. Place an order for "US Paracentesis".

    Consider ED observation admission for Procedure Service vs. holding in the ED till AM for Procedure Service. The ED portion of the pathway is complete.
